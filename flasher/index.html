Web Serial cannot flash nRF52 boards. Use the UF2 bootloader drag-and-drop method below.

How to flash the Heltec T114

  1. Double-tap RESET quickly — the board enters UF2 bootloader mode. A USB drive named T114BOOT (or similar) appears on your computer.
  2. Click Download UF2 below to save the firmware file.
  3. Drag the downloaded .uf2 file onto the USB drive.
  4. The drive disappears and the board reboots automatically into the new firmware.
